Venusuar フシギバナ #0003

›

Venusaur is a large, plant-like Pokémon recognized for the giant blooming flower on its back, which radiates a calming, fragrant aroma. Standing on four sturdy legs with a thick, green hide, Venusaur uses its plant's broad leaves and roots to draw energy from the sun, powering up its potent Grass-type attacks. The flower can release powerful pollen and toxic spores, which Venusaur uses strategically in battle to disorient or poison its foes. Despite its massive size and strength, Venusaur is known for its balanced nature, often acting as a guardian of the forest, nurturing the plants and Pokémon around it.
