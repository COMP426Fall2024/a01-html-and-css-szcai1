Charizard is a powerful, dragon-like Pokémon known for its fiery breath and impressive aerial abilities. Standing on two legs with a long tail that ends in a flame, Charizard is orange with a pair of large wings that enable it to fly at great speeds. It has a rugged snout, a horn-like crest on its head, and sharp claws on its hands and feet. Charizard's flame burns hotter when it is furious, and it can melt anything in its path with its intense fire breath. It is a highly skilled flyer and often soars high in the skies searching for powerful opponents to battle. Despite its fierce appearance and capabilities, Charizard is known to be proud and noble, never using its flames against weaker opponents.
